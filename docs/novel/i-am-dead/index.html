Synopsis

“I’d rather die.”

For 10 years.

I became the daughter-in-law of a chaebol family, and for ten long years, I endured the vile insults of my scumbag husband and the disdainful gaze of my mother-in-law, who treated me like a mere housekeeper.

Still, I was filled with hope. I dreamt of a future where I could escape this place and realize my dreams…

Then, an unexpected accident claimed my life, just as that bastard had predicted.

—

“Miss! Are you out of your mind?”

When I opened my eyes, the reflection in the mirror wasn’t mine—it was my perfect sister-in-law, the one who had everything.

I’m sorry, girl. I’m going to have it all with this body.

I’m dead.
