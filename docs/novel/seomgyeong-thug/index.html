Synopsis

It was still raining in the unfamiliar city of Seomgyeong.

Shim Hongju was simply trying to find shelter from the rain. She didn’t have any other plans.

“What are you doing? Do you even know me?”

That’s when Lee Eunsan abruptly barged into her everyday life.

“Does it matter? You’re pretty, I’m pretty, so let’s grab a meal and have se.x together when we have time.”

Hongju was taken aback by this unfamiliar human interaction—a first in her life.

But why?

As days passed, this brazen thug starts to look like a man…

‘You’ve fallen into a trap.’

In that moment, a sharp voice jolted Hongju from her sweet slumber.
