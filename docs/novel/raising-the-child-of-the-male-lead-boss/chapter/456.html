The fans didn’t stop there. They skimmed the posts from several years ago which accused Yu Yaoyao of cheating and found that Yu Xinjie had liked and bumped up the posts to defame Yu Yaoyao. They shamed her for being a ‘wolf under a sister’s skin’.

In the duplex villa, Yu Xinjie broke down and slipped on the floor, crying and trembling. “Mom, what should I do, what should I do…,” she stammered, sobbing, “I can’t face people anymore.”

“Xinjie, listen to me,” said Zhao Mei, “You still have a little bit of money left. Go abroad for a few years until this goes down. Your sister has ruined your reputation. People just need time to forget or turn to something else. We won’t pay the money back; you will go live abroad where no one knows you. Come back in two years’ time….” Zhao Mei was trying to hug her. But Yu Xinjie pushed her away.

“Mom!” she exclaimed, “What are you even saying? If we don’t pay everything back, brother-in-law will sue me! Anybody could find out the lawsuit in my name with a simple internet search. Even if I go abroad, no one in the Chinese district will treat me well.”

“I don’t even have the amount that it takes to make a living abroad!” Yu Xinjie continued, “Father’s company doesn’t earn so much either. We will need to sell the house and the beauty salon to pay back.”

Zhao Mei frowned; she looked unwilling. Yu Xinjie noticed this. She looked at her mother and tears flowed anew from her eyes.

“Mummy,” she said in disbelief, “Even if I were to be sued, you won’t sell the house and the salon to pay them back?”

Zhao Mei sneered. “You brat! What are you even saying?” she said, “We don’t have anything! We don’t have any money at home. It was your sister who gave us the money voluntarily. Didn’t you say we don’t even need to pay it back when you last received it? Will it even be a legal issue anyway?!”

Yu Xinjie shook her head in disbelief. This was her mother? She covered her face in her face and heaved a breath. She stood up and pushed Zhao Mei aside and rushed to her bedroom.

“Where are the real estate documents?” she said, searching frantically through the drawers, “Where is the registration certificate for the beauty salon? I am going to sell it all and pay them back!”
