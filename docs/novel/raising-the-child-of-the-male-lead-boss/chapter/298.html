Shen Yichong quickly couldn’t understand Yu Yaoyao’s motivation to learn English. He never knew that one could learn English like this… He never had to worry about his son’s study, nor was he a student that his teachers had to worry about, which was why he doubted if he had read the wrong textbook when it came to Yu Yaoyao.

Little Bun could attend the online school and follow the teacher’s teaching. He would start from simple words and daily sentences, and he gradually improved from that foundation. He would only ask him on occasion when he really didn’t know some vocabulary.

As for Yu Yaoyao, Shen Yichong would feel a pain in his temples every time she asked a question.

But then she said in their mother tongue, “Darling, how about we do it tonight?”

Deep into the night was the best time to be romantic with each other. Shen Yichong was also a little moved.

However, in the next sentence, Yu Yaoyao asked, “How do you say this sentence in English?”

Suddenly, he felt as if a basin of cold water was poured down on his head. Immediately, all his excited feelings were gone. Not only that, but he also had to help her translate.

Otherwise, she would look at him excitedly, “Oh, I didn’t know there’s vocabulary that you also don’t know!”

Shen Yichong can only pray that his little girl would not be like her mother. If she had really taken after her mother, he could only pray for his future son-in-law. He wouldn’t be able to help him either!

Regarding the fact that his wife would always make learning English s*xual…Shen Yichong had no choice but to correct her a few times. But he gave up in correcting her when he found that she would always raise even weirder questions after that.

As long as she was happy. After all, she was his wife. He has to answer her no matter what. It was infinitely better than having her asking other people.
