Chapter 105 – Not So Chaste

This fox is very cunning in her colorful ways!

“Elder sister, if you don’t confess, reporters will …”

The crowd looked at Meng Xinran’s eyes with eyes full of doubt.

Meng Xinran’s heart thumped and clenched her fist. “Just from his tattoos, one can already see that he is not a chaste man!”

If they did that, the sheets should have left the scent of their lovemaking!

The man should have worn a c*nd0m! Yes, that’s right! Before he left, he must have disposed of the used c*nd0m in this room!

All the colors of Meng Xinran’s face disappeared in a few seconds!
