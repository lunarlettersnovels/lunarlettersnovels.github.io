Chapter 75

Hero knew Freya was a good person, but for him, that wasn’t enough.

 

It’s no use turning her into a noble person out of nowhere.

 

Freya’s body, leaning on Lucius’ shoulder, shook slightly. Hero’s face was dyed with anxiety by the appearance of the two sitting side by side.

 

The carriage was rattling. Perhaps passing through the bumpy floor, Freya’s butt kept bouncing upward.

 

I just pretended to be asleep and now I can hear my snore already.

 

Freya frowned because she felt uncomfortable all over. The window of the wagon shook as if it would break.

 

I feel weird.

 

This was because she felt the warm temperature on her cheeks. When she opened her eyes slightly, she felt herself leaning her head against Lucius’ shoulder.

 

“Oh my! I’m really out of my mind.”

 

It was a dizzying moment to dare to lean on the Prince’s body and fell asleep. She started raising her head slowly.

 

But somehow it was hard to move her head because Lucius’ hand was on top of it.

 

Oh, I’m going crazy.

 

A cold sweat began to flow on her back. Once she pushed his hand aside, then she could raise her head. Freya stretched her arms high and tried to remove his hands, and she felt a hot gaze.

 

“Did you want to touch my hand that much?”

 

“Oh…”

 

Having made eye contact, Lucius said something absurd.

 

“That’s not what you think, your highness…”

 

It was true that Freya leaned on his shoulder. But holding his hands was not intended to be like that. Lucius smiled lightly as she blushed her cheeks out of anger at being misunderstood.

 

“It’s okay. It’s not a shame that you fell in love with me.”

 

“Ughhhh….”

 

Freya realized that no matter what she said, she could not be cleared of the false accusation. She suddenly became a person who fell in love with Lucius.

 

“Let’s not talk.” Just giving up, Freya hoped to get off the wagon quickly.

 

Hero, who fell asleep deeply, was not helpful, and sitting next to Lucius, who stared at her the whole time, was pain itself.

 

Freya, who diverted her attention at the outside scenery, was mesmerized.

 

“It’s really incredible!” As they passed by the magnificent gate, there was a line of beautiful buildings with sparkling windows. Freya looked up at the house planted with rose vines on the balcony, and the scent of flowers crossed her nose.

 

Is there really a place like this?

 

When she sat on a warm street begging, Freya thought she knew the world quite well, but now seeing these things, she almost laughed in vain.

 

For a while, there was tension on Freya’s face again.

 

It’s not the time to be watching like this.

 

Freya was on her way to the palace. It wasn’t just going, it was almost like being caught.

 

There’s no way I’ll do well.

 

Fear flooded in like a tsunami, and Freya bit her lips tightly.

 

“Freya, your face is so pale.” Hero, who was awakened was worried about her.

 

She asked Hero for it with a look of regret as much as possible.

 

” Can we stop the carriage for a second?”

 

Freya was obsessed with a strong hunch that she shouldn’t enter the palace like this.

 

You have to run away when you have a chance.

 

She felt sorry for Archer, but she is just such a selfish human being. Hero nodded to her tired face, too.

 

“I think I really need to get some fresh air outside.”

 

“Your Highness, what should I do?”

 

“There will be no stopping of carriage. Freya, stick your head out of the window to breathe fresh air.” Lucius was calm even as he watched her tremble.

 

“It’s because I’m getting motion sickness”. Freya answered.

 

It wasn’t a lie. She was so nervous that she felt motion sickness that she didn’t have. However, the crown prince seemed to have no intention of listening to her at all.

 

“Freya, it’s better to quit.” Lucius, sitting with his long legs crossed, winked.

 

“What? Like you’re looking inside my head.” Did he notice Freya’s plan to hide through the alleys when she stopped the carriage? She begged again with a pathetic look.

 

“I feel like throwing up, Your Highness.”

 

“You can throw up in the carriage as much as you want. I’ll allow this.”

 

“In the carriage?”

 

Freya felt like her head was going to explode because she couldn’t communicate at all.

 

I’m not confident that I’ll be doing well today, let alone the remaining nine months.

 

Leaning towards her, he whispered in her ear.

 

“In addition, let’s slowly kiss each other.”

 

“What?”

 

Freya, whose eyes were wide open, jumped from her seat. Those words made Freya’s head dizzy.

 

“You don’t even remember what happened in the cave, do you?”’

 

She stuttered her lips with one hand, blurring the end of her speech.

 

“Miss de Bua of Del Mundo.”

 

Just by listening to Lucius, he spoke of a title that seemed to cause the game.

 

“Your highness, I don’t think that’s possible at all.”
