Hero has had a lot of training and has shown that he is always willing to do what he is told.

Even Gemini, the most trustworthy knight, was at Lucius’ side.

“However, why am I so anxious?”

He isn’t even a helpless child who couldn’t save her mother until the very end. Even a brief respite brought him so much pain.

“Your Majesty, would you prefer medication?”

“No, it’s okay. Is there any other messenger like the one we sent?”

“Thankfully not?”

It was instructed to send a notification whenever something occurred at the palace.

“Is there an issue?”

Gemini inquired while seeing Lucius’ countenance, which was impossibly black. Given that the silent subordinate closed his mouth, the utterance must have been quite impressive. The embarrassed Lucius patted Gemini’s shoulder and nodded, assuring him that everything was fine.

“I can’t sleep because I’m so enthusiastic about the impending conclusion.”

A soldier standing next to Lucius smiled and shook his shoulders.

“I suppose my rumors have not changed.”

“I believe it will be simple for at least the next decade.”

Lucius, who was laughing meanly at Gemini’s joke, turned his eyes to the palace in the distance.

“Before going to the palace, I will visit a specific location.”

He intended to purchase Freya a white-cream-filled cake. On each journey back to the palace, Lucius stopped at a bakery to purchase cookies or bread. She has no idea how proud he was whenever he carried out a neatly wrapped box.

All misunderstandings will be cleared up if you apologize repeatedly and communicate appropriately.

Freya is the only one who has ever held his hand, both then and now.

His blue eyes, which were momentarily lost in meditation, became progressively cooler. He gathered his golden hair with his hand and then opened his mouth.

“Leader, let us now depart.”

“I’ll take orders.”

Gemini told the soldiers to get back to work when Lucius put on the helmet he had taken off.
