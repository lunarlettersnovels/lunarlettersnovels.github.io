Synopsis

Jasmine had an accident with her one-sided love, Duke Aion. The straightforward man, who didn’t bat an eye at her before, who consistently ignored her, for some reason, was now catching feelings…

“Didn’t you say you like me? Was that a lie?”

“Why would I lie about that?”

She was at that phase where she decided to end her d@mned unrequited love, but instead, it turned for the worse. What comes next is an enchanting love story between a noble couple at each other’s throats but in harmony during the night.
