Announcement

Hello everyone, we hope this message finds you well.

After some discussion, we’ve decided to drop Saving the Prince Regent (SPR). We’ve initially chosen the story for its uniqueness (reversal of roles: female lead is more dependable, takes care of male lead). However, it has spiraled out of control in later chapters where the female lead begins to take advantage of the male lead, without asking for his consent (i.e. groping his body, forcing him into a marriage..)

No matter if it’s a man or woman, it’s an immoral thing to do. The translators find it difficult to proceed with the story without feeling uncomfortable. We also believe in giving our readers worthwhile stories.

In place, Raising the Child of the Male Lead Boss (RCM) and The Empress Returns (TER)  will have more releases at 4-6 chapters a week.

We apologize to the readers and thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,

Lunar letters
