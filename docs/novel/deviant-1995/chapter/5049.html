Arriving at the hotel around dawn, André washed off the sweet scent that clung to his body with scalding hot water. He firmly resolved never to seek out Miran again.

But when it was time for Miran to get off work, André found himself in the small alleyways of Poidong without realizing it. Like a loyal dog waiting for its owner, he stood by a tall utility pole, waiting for her.

Even while waiting, his mind changed dozens of times. He would turn to leave, thinking about what he was doing, but when Miran spotted him from the end of the alley and ran towards him with a bright, sunny smile, he would ultimately sigh in resignation and succumb to his desires.

André de Lafayette, who had always been stricter with himself than anyone else, gradually became accustomed to this deviation. The pretext for their meetings was script practice. But once inside the small rooftop room, no one mentioned the script.

The afternoon before the day of the film shoot.

It was a day when Miran’s part-time job ended earlier than usual. In the late afternoon, André stood in front of the utility pole as usual. The surroundings were still bright since the sun had not yet set. As a result, things he couldn’t see in the dark night became noticeable.

Smelly garbage bags left outside the gate, dirty posters, and scattered flyers on the ground.

André let out a heavy sigh. One of the things the pitch-black darkness had hidden was André himself, who was swept away by desire and came here every day.

He rubbed his face with his hands and looked up at the clear blue sky. He knew that Miran liked him. She looked at him with such blind affection that even a stranger could notice it. André didn’t dislike her either. But that was all.

With the film shoot scheduled for tomorrow, there would be no more excuses or reasons to come here. So today would truly be the last time he came here.

Miran blinked blankly, and the moment she understood his words, her cheeks flared up. She quickly turned around, opened the back door, and disappeared inside as if fleeing.

His thick, long eyelashes lowered slightly as he extended his arms to encircle her, interlocking his soapy hands with hers. The soap slipped and fell into the sink.

“Ugh…”
