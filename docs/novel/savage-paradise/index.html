Synopsis

Lee Goyo is a fortunate orphan, though her luck takes a dark turn.

Fourteen years ago, she was rescued alongside the prosecutor’s son from a kidnapping, finding herself under the care of Lee Eui-taek, who was then the deputy chief prosecutor.

However, instead of a sweet reward, she held onto the handle of hell’s gate.

In return for being part of Lee Eui-taek’s family, Lee Goyo became their pawn and pet.

“Lee Eui-taek instructed you to seduce me, didn’t he?”

“If I do, will you fall for it?”

To survive, she must constantly prove her worth. Even if it means seducing someone who had nothing to do with her– KJ Financial’s CEO, Kwon Jae-heon.

“Lee Goyo, what do you want?” Kwon Jae-heon asked her.

“Why? Can you grant it?”

“What do you desire? Tell me sincerely, no jokes.”

“What I desire is…”

Only freedom. Despite her struggles, there seems no escape from this personal hell.

“Just promise me one thing. Promise to rescue me from them.”

She’d gladly accept even a poisoned apple.

“I said I’d kill for you. Don’t you believe me?”

In that moment, to Yo, that dangerously savage man seemed like a savior leading her to paradise.
