I recalled Kaichen showing interest in my occasional remarks about my past life as I spoke. He seemed to quite like the term ‘healing.’

In this world, it refers to the magic used to recover one’s body, but back in my old world, they used it to describe taking it easy and relaxing. Resting to heal the mind. Since then, he would seriously say to me, “Dalia, let’s go for healing.” How amusing it was!

Kaichen continued to gaze at me intently with a raised eyebrow.

“I have to wash up and get ready now…” I said.

I glanced back. The chefs couldn’t bring themselves to touch the perfectly prepared dishes. They busily discussed among themselves with notebooks in hand, nodding in agreement.

“Dalia, you…”
