Sunlight poured through the window, casting streaks across Heeju’s face. Her eyelids, which had been closed as if she were dead, twitched slightly, and after a moment, she slowly opened her eyes.

She blinked at the ceiling blankly before slowly sitting up in bed. Something that had been on her forehead fell down. It was a square cooling patch that had been stuck to her forehead, now lying on the blanket.

Aah, yesterday…

But did Kwon Gukhyun put that on me?

After setting down the empty water bottle, Heeju glanced at the medicine packaging rolling around on the side table. She then touched her forehead with her hand. Unlike her throat, which felt as dry as if it would tear, only a slight fever remained in her head.
