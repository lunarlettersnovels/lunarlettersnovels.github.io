Synopsis

I became the villainess stepmother who is doomed to suffer greatly at the hands of the child, who will return as a tyrant due to his years of child abuse. 

I can’t die like this. No, I was determined to survive!

However… Why does avoiding this gruesome fate seem so easy?

And, to make matters worse, the male lead began to obsess over me.
