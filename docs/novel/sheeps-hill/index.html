Synopsis

※ This work is set in the 1980s. All institutions, professions, regions, events, and characters depicted are entirely fictional.

 

No. 1 on the music charts more times than anyone else.
A record-breaking 14 weeks at the top.
The most admired woman.
The sexiest female singer.

She was Geummi, the dance-pop queen who swept every title imaginable.
But one day, after losing consciousness, she wakes up in the body of inmate No. 89-7059, Ham Yeohee, inside Cheongjin Women’s Prison.

I have to get back to my real body!

But with no money, no connections, and no power, Ham Yeohee is dismissed as a lunatic.
Just as she struggles to find a way out, luck arrives in the form of Officer Ki.

“I want to see yours, Officer Ki.”
“……”
“Would it be wrong if I said I liked you?”
“If you’re sure you won’t regret it, then go ahead.”

The plan worked. The stone-faced man was finally reacting.

“I’ll use you up and toss you aside when I’m done, you know.”

But then—

“If you get pregnant, I’ll take responsibility.”

Instead of letting her go, the man reveals an overwhelming obsession for her.

 

—

“You said you’d only show me, but you spread yourself for anyone.”

The long, rounded tip of his baton pressed between my legs.

“If you keep leaking everywhere like that…”
“…ahh…”

He nudged the baton against the sensitive spot again and again, then ground it firmly into me.

“What do you expect me to do?”
